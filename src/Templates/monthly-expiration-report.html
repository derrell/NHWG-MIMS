Dear Commander, Deputy, Personnel and Recruiting Officers:

Attached are the monthly automated wing wide membership expiration reports. One report lists all of the wing memberships that have expired in the previous 90-days. The second report lists all wing memberships that will be expiring at the end of the month. The third report lists all wing memberships expiring two months out. These reports are provide so Commanders, Personnel and Recruiting Officers may be pro-active in member retention. The reports are sorted by squadron and provided as spreadsheet files for your use.

The reports are provided to all Commanders, Personnel, and Recruiting Officers in order to give a view into the total membership trends for the entire wing.
